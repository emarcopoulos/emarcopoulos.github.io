A Biography of Elias Marcopoulos

Elias was born in 1997 and grew up in a small town in New Jersey called Park Ridge. He went to West Ridge Elementary School and then later on Park Ridge High School. During his time at PRHS, Elias was a member of many groups: He was an actor in the theatre programs, a long distance runner for the track teams, a clarinet player and drum major of the marching band, a basketball player, an origami teacher for elementary enrichment, the president of his local GOYA, and an altar boy for St. Athanasios Greek Orthodox Church.

Graduating from high school in 2015, Elias then enrolled at Tufts University. There, Elias spent time working as a dishwasher and as a TA for Intro to Computer Science. He also played the clarinet for the pep band and coded games just for fun. Elias plans on graduating from the School of Engineering in 2019 with a degree in Computer Science and a degree in English.
