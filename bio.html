I grew up in a small town in New Jersey called Park Ridge. I went to West Ridge Elementary School and then later on Park Ridge High School. During my time at PRHS, I was a member of many groups: I was an actor in the theatre programs, a long distance runner for the track teams, a clarinet player and drum major for the marching band, a basketball player, an origami teacher for elementary enrichment, the president of the local GOYA, and an altar person for St. Athanasios Greek Orthodox Church.

Graduating from high school in 2015, I then enrolled at Tufts University. There, I spent time working as a dishwasher and as a TA for Intro to Computer Science (and later more advanced courses!). I played the clarinet and became president of the pep band. I often enjoyed coding games just for fun. I graduated from the School of Engineering in 2019 with degrees in Computer Science, English and Physics.

I did four internships while at Tufts and have since graduating worked two jobs full time!
