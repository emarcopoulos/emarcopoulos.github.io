A Biography of Elias Marcopoulos

Elias was born in 1997 and grew up in a small town in New Jersey called Park Ridge. He went to West Ridge Elementary School and then later on Park Ridge High School. Graduating from high school in 2015, Elias then enrolled at Tufts University.

At Tufts University, Elias plans on graduating from the School of Engineering in 2019 with a degree in Computer Science and a degree in English.
